Brief Mention of SOLID  
- https://deviq.com/solid/
- https://www.tomdalling.com/blog/category/software-design/

Single Responsibility Principle  
- Each method should have its own single responsibility

Open Closed Principle  
- Programs should be open to extension but closed to modification

Liskov Substitution Principle  
- Subclasses should be substituble for base classes. 
- https://www.tomdalling.com/blog/software-design/solid-class-design-the-liskov-substitution-principle/

Interface Integration Principle  
- Tailor interfaces to what the individual client needs. Smaller interfaces so that they can be fully implemented and not break the LSP. 

Dependency Inversion Principle  
- high level modules should not depend on low level modules
- https://stackify.com/dependency-inversion-principle/
